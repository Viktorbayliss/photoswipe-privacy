Privacy Policy

PhotoSwipe Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app accesses your photo library only to allow you to review and delete photos and videos directly on your device.

All processing happens locally on your device. No photos, videos, or personal information are uploaded to external servers.

PhotoSwipe Pro does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer.
